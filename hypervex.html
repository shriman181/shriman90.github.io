The Problem

A massive credit gap — built on broken tools.

Microfinance institutions across South Asia, Sub-Saharan Africa, and Latin America are lending at scale with spreadsheet-era tools — no machine learning, no real-time risk signals, and no credit bureau coverage for most borrowers.

01
Thin-File Borrowers, Zero Data
Over 140 million microfinance borrowers have no formal credit history. Traditional scoring models return blanks. Loan officers rely on gut instinct and paper ledgers — creating inconsistent, biased, and slow decisions.
02
Loan Officer Productivity Collapse
MFI loan officers spend 60–70% of their time on manual data gathering, field visits, and form-filling. Decision turnaround times stretch to days or weeks. Portfolio quality degrades as scale increases.
03
Silent Portfolio Deterioration
Portfolio At Risk (PAR) deterioration often goes undetected until it's too late. MFIs lack early-warning systems to flag at-risk borrowers before repayment failures cascade into write-offs.
04
Alternative Data, Untapped
Mobile money transactions, utility bill regularity, airtime top-up patterns — all are powerful creditworthiness signals sitting unused. No existing MFI tool aggregates and models this data at scale.

Founder

The person behind Hypervex.

KS
Krithick Santhosh
Founder & CEO, Hypervex
"A billion people move money on their phones every day. That data tells a complete story about who can be trusted with credit. We just built the engine to read it."

Krithick Santhosh founded Hypervex after seeing first-hand how the fundamental infrastructure of credit — the ability to prove creditworthiness — remained completely inaccessible to the world's most financially active informal economies.

With deep roots in financial systems and a conviction that alternative data is the definitive unlock for financial inclusion at scale, Krithick built Hypervex around a simple thesis: mobile money networks in Sub-Saharan Africa, South Asia, and Southeast Asia already contain more predictive credit signal than most bureau files in developed markets. The tools to extract that signal just didn't exist yet.

Hypervex is Krithick's answer to that gap — a purpose-built credit intelligence layer that puts institutional-grade underwriting in the hands of every microfinance institution, community development finance institution, and fintech lender serving the world's underserved majority.
